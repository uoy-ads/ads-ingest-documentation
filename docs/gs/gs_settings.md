# Settings

The Settings menu is accessed via the top right hand corner of the Dashboard. In this menu you can set default information about [Copyright Holders](../nc/nc_people.md), [Web Contact](../nc/nc_people.md) and [Invoice details](../nc/nc_invoice.md).

By inputting this information into the Settings menu you ensure that you do not have to re-enter these details for every collection. The default options can also be edited within these pages if required. Follow the links above to find out further information about each field.

## Default Copyright Holder

Please enter details about who is the default copyright holder of your collections. This may include the original author or the organisation who is responsible for creating the content. If you have multiple copyright holders, additional information can be added on the [People & Organisations page](../nc/nc_people.md).

To add details about the copyright holder first select the ‘Type’ \- either a ‘Person’ or ‘Organisation’. 

If you selected a person you will need to complete the following fields:

* First Name  
* Last Name  
* ORCID \- Please enter the full URL including unique sixteen digit Open Researcher and Contributor ID (ORCiD) reference, e.g. https://orcid.org/0000-0000-0000-0000

If you selected ‘Organisation’ you will only need to enter the Organisation name. 

## Default Web Contact

Please enter information about who users should contact if they have a question about your collection. This name and email address will be publicly available alongside your collection when it is published. A Web Contact can be a Person or an Organisation. 

If you are depositing on behalf of an organisation, you may wish to include a general email address rather than the contact details of a specific person.

Please note, if you do enter the details of a specific person under web contact, please ensure you receive their permission to put their contact details on our website prior to submitting your collection.

To add details about the Web Contact first select the ‘Type’ \- either a ‘Person’ or ‘Organisation’. 

If you selected ‘Person’ you will need to complete the following fields:

* First Name   
* Last Name   
* ORCID \- Please enter the full URL including unique sixteen digit Open Researcher and Contributor ID (ORCiD) reference, e.g. https://orcid.org/0000-0000-0000-0000
* Email address

If you selected ‘Organisation’, you will need to complete the following fields:

* Organisation Name \- Type in the field to search available options. If the organisation that you are looking for does not appear, please type in the name manually.  
* Email address

## Default Invoice Details

Please enter contact details associated with invoicing for the cost of your deposition. You should enter the contact details of a person who is responsible for ensuring the payment of invoices so as to not delay the deposition and publication of your collection.

* Name (first and last name)- Please enter the first and last name for your nominated contact.   
* Organisation- If applicable, please include the name of your organisation.  
* Email \- Please enter the email address for your nominated contact.   
* Address \- Please enter the physical address associated with your organisation or nominated contact.
