# Code of Conduct

All ADS and HSDS Training events are subject to a Code of Conduct. 

We are committed to creating a friendly, respectful and harassment-free experience for learning. 

We encourage all participants (instructor and learners) to act in the following manner and show respect and courtesy to others at all times.

* Use welcoming and inclusive language
* Be respectful of different viewpoints and experiences
* Show courtesy and respect towards other participants

If you have any issues please report it to your instructor (via Zoom Direct Message or email) and we will address it as a matter of priority. If you have any complaints please contact the ADS helpdesk (help@archaeologydataservice.ac.uk) and we ensure to address all issues.
