# Ingest Q & A Drop-in Sessions

Drop-in sessions are open, informal Q&A sessions for Ingest users. Come with your questions — whether about your own datasets, your organisation's workflows, or anything you weren't sure about after training. There are no presentations or set agenda; the session is led entirely by what you need.

> 💡 **New to Ingest?** We recommend attending an [Introduction to Ingest training session](../tr/tr_intro_ingest.md) before joining a drop-in. Drop-ins are designed for users who have completed basic training and want to go deeper with their own datasets.

---

## Session Details

💻 Format: Online via Zoom

⏱️ Duration: 1 hour

👥 Audience: Ingest users who have completed the 'Introduction to Ingest' basic training

---

## Upcoming Sessions

All sessions are online via Zoom, 10:00 AM–11:00 PM. See the [Training Homepage](../tr/tr_index.md) for a full list of upcoming sessions.

---

**What you can ask about:**

- Working with your own datasets and deposits
- Metadata requirements specific to your project or organisation
- File formats, validation errors, and technical issues
- How Ingest connects to other ADS systems (UMA, Archive Interface, Data Catalogue)
- Anything you weren't sure about after the introductory training

**How your questions help others:**
Common questions and themes raised in drop-in sessions are recorded and fed back into future training sessions and documentation — so your questions help improve the experience for all users.

## Submit a Question in Advance

You can send questions ahead of the session to ensure the right team members are prepared to answer.

👉 Email: [help@archaeologydataservice.ac.uk](mailto:help@archaeologydataservice.ac.uk) with the subject line: *Drop-in question — [date of session]*

## Before You Attend

- Complete the [Introduction to Ingest training session](../tr/tr_intro_ingest.md) if you haven't already
- Register for your chosen session using the links above
- Have your Ingest login ready — you may want to share your screen during the session
- Bring your questions, or submit them in advance!
